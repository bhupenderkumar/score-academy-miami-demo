Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for young learners, with a focus on play-based learning, social skills, and emotional intelligence.

📚

Elementary

Our elementary program builds on the fundamentals of reading, writing, math, and science, with an emphasis on critical thinking and problem-solving.

🔍

Middle/Upper

Our middle and upper school programs offer a challenging curriculum, with courses in STEM, humanities, and the arts, as well as opportunities for leadership and community service.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program is designed for students who are passionate about science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, with courses in coding, robotics, and engineering design.

🎨

Arts

Our arts program offers courses in music, visual arts, and drama, with opportunities for students to express themselves creatively and develop their skills in performance and exhibition.

🏋️‍♀️

Athletics

Our athletics program offers a range of sports teams for students in grades 4-8, with a focus on teamwork, sportsmanship, and physical fitness.
