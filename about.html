Our Mission & History

Score Academy Miami was founded in 2005 with a vision to provide a high-quality, personalized education to students in Miami's vibrant community. Our school has grown to serve over 200 students in grades PreK-8, with a focus on academic excellence and character development.

Our teaching approach is centered on project-based learning, where students engage in hands-on activities that promote critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration. Our experienced teachers guide students in exploring their interests and passions, while fostering a love of learning that lasts a lifetime.

At Score Academy Miami, we believe in the importance of extracurricular activities and character development in shaping well-rounded individuals. Our students participate in sports teams, clubs, and volunteer programs that help them build friendships, develop new skills, and give back to their community.
